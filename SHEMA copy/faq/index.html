Frequently Asked Questions

Have a question about SHEMA, our approach, or how to use the app? Explore the answers below —or contact us if you don’t find what you’re looking for.

General

What is SHEMA?

SHEMA is an AI‑powered Bible study tool that highlights the Jewish roots of Scripture so you can understand passages in their original context.

Who is SHEMA for?

Anyone seeking a deeper grasp of the Bible—from individuals and small groups to pastors and curious explorers—can benefit from SHEMA.

Theological

Why focus on Jewish context?

The Bible was written within the culture of ancient Israel. Knowing that heritage reveals nuances and connections that strengthen faith today.

Does SHEMA support both Old and New Testaments?

Yes. SHEMA explores insights from Genesis through Revelation, connecting both Testaments through their shared Jewish roots.

Is this app for a specific denomination?

No. SHEMA is non‑denominational and welcomes believers from any tradition who wish to learn.

Technical

Which devices are supported?

You can access SHEMA on modern browsers across mobile phones, tablets, and desktop computers.

Is my data secure?

We respect your privacy. All information is transmitted over encrypted connections and stored securely.

How do I report a bug or suggest a feature?

Send us an email at info@rootedinlove.org and we’ll respond as soon as possible.

Don’t see your question?

Contact Us and we’ll get back to you.
