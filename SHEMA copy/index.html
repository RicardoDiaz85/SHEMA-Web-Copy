Frequently Asked Questions

Why is Jewish context important for Bible study?

Jewish context uncovers the rabbinic meanings behind Jesus’ teachings, the rich Hebrew imagery, idioms, and customs of His time that are woven throughout Scripture. Most Western translations only skim the surface, missing references, parables, remez (hidden clues), and everyday expressions that were obvious to His original Jewish audience. Studying the Bible this way invites you to go deeper than what you’ve ever been taught, discovering layers of meaning, culture, and connections that transform how you read and live out God’s Word.

How does SHEMA’s AI provide Jewish commentary and insight?

SHEMA’s AI is trained on Messianic Jewish sources, rabbinic wisdom, and trusted scholarship. It delivers chapter-by-chapter insights that highlight Jewish language, culture, history, and interpretation, helping you see Scripture through its original lens.

What makes SHEMA different from other Bible apps?

SHEMA uniquely combines AI technology with authentic Jewish and Messianic Jewish teaching. It lets you ask questions, explore every chapter, and uncover the Bible as Jesus and His first followers would have understood it—beyond Western traditions or filters.
